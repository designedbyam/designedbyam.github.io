Race Type

The Beast is a half-marathon distance race that involves challenging terrain and significant elevation changes, making it a grueling endurance event.

The 2019 Asia Pacific Championship was held at the Thaiwoo Ski Resort in Zhangjiakou, China. Placing 1st, 2nd, or 3rd qualifies you for the 2019 Spartan World Championship in North Lake Tahoe, California.

Detail Information
Date August 25, 2019
Location Zhangjiakou, China
Distance 21 km
Elevation Gain 1,800 meters (5,900 feet)
Number of Obstacles 33
Weather Cool, variable — started hazy, overcast later

Main Challenges

  1. Steep and continuous uphill climbs
  2. Rocky and slippery descents
  3. Obstacles requiring upper body strength and agility
  4. Dealing with cramps and fatigue
  5. Language barrier for support and communication

Race Highlights

  • First Spartan Beast race completed
  • Overcame intense leg cramps 8 times
  • Earned the coveted Trifecta wedge
  • Finished 2nd place overall
